Happy birthday to one of the most genuine souls I’ve ever known.

I don’t know if you’ll ever know who this is…
but I hope you feel it.

I just wanted to say that you’ve always been someone really special to me.

You changed something in my life without even trying… you made me see things differently,
made life feel a little lighter, a little better.

There’s something about you that feels real in a way that’s hard to find.

You’re not like everyone else, and I hope you never become like them.

I won’t talk about the moments we had… I think I’ll keep those to myself.
But they meant more than you know.

Your laugh

You’ll always be someone important to me, no matter what happens.

but I’ll always remember you.

Some people leave a mark on your life…
and you’re one of them.
